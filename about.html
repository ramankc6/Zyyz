About

A conscious positive environment action (CPEA) is any choice you make, consciously, during the day that has a positive effect on our environment, regardless of size. You will be able to keep track of every CPEA that you do during the day, week, or however else long you have it set to. Along with keeping track of CPEAs, ZyZZ will also provide incentives (shareable trophies and notifications) at specific points of achievement during the set time period. We intend on using this app in order to help boost people's enviromental awareness through simple incentives on their phone.
